BNB LIBRARY- TRIVANDURM

"Books N Beyond" library is the place you can walk straight to without having to worry about starting or maintaining a membership fee. Reading a book is completely free here. You can just walk in, read the book, and give it back when you leave. If you wish to take the book home, you need to furnish a security deposit, which will be duly refunded when the book is returned. "Books N Beyond" is the ideal location for those who love to immerse themselves in the world of reading

Sukesh Ramakrishna Pillai is the founder and CEO of Love All Sports Academy and a resident of Trivandrum. He considers the "Books N Beyond" Library as the fruition of his dream. For Sukesh Ramakrishna Pillai, reading is an emotion. The sole aim of the Books and Beyond library is to nurture a habit of reading, which the younger generation seems to have lost. Anyone who enters the Books and Beyond library can take a book and start reading. This library housed nearly, 9000 books, the majority of which were contributed by book lovers.

It's possible that the majority of the less fortunate kids in our community lack access to books they can read and grow up with. The fundamental objective of the Books N Beyond Library is to make books available to anyone who wants to read and learn.

 


 

The idea of the Book N Beyond library took off when Sukesh R Pillai spoke to his two childhood friends, Asok P and Prakash M A. Books N Beyond Library imprinted its name in the heart of every book lover. He created a number of plans using the library platform, including organizing frequent storytelling sessions. The library includes a variety of literature for readers of all ages, from comics to reference books. They also have a great sitting area that is peaceful and quiet to allow for immersive studying. Additionally, a "Games cafeteria" is provided inside the campus, where people can play chess, Ludo, snakes and ladders, and many other interesting board games. Your game will be under the surveillance of your favourite and most classic cartoon characters, Boban and Molly, Suppandi, Shikari Shambu, and Mowgli, all of whom were decorated on the walls of the cafeteria.

Thiruvananthapuram has a serious crowd of readers, and the capital city formerly had a thriving culture that valued reading. This was evident in many ways. Children these days are glued to screens. The Books and Beyond Library is designed to foster their skills. Reading helps a great deal with building our confidence, reducing stress, and putting us in a better mood. It also expands our understanding and enriches the growth of our creativity. Books are indeed our best friends because reading develops our knowledge and critical thinking skills.

Author & CEO of Love All Sports and BNB library.
