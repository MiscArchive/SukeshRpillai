Book Donation Drive

With a single mission, we are hosting a book donation drive and helping to start a community library in Trivandrum. Spark a reading revolution today itself. We are conducting a campaign “Book Donation Drive for Tribal Child-2023”.

We want to set up a library for Tribal Community in Trivandrum. To do this we aim to collect more than 300 books in Malayalam and English by to be used in this library. We are trying to organise a massive week-long book-collecting and donation drive in each cone of Trivandrum city. Support us by spreading the word around, for collecting the maximum number of books for the community library.




Most underprivileged children in our area will not be able to afford to learn. However, by giving away your books, you can give them access to materials that are normally reserved for a particular group of people. You'll change the nation's power imbalance in your own small manner. Additionally, since textbooks are the most expensive, donating them will allow you to actively contribute to someone's education.

Donating is a nice and generous act that spreads goodwill across society. The benefits of contribution may be multiplied if others in your area are motivated by your action to follow suit. Through reinforcement, this will bring you and the people around you inner peace and encourage more book donations. As this cycle continues, you'll come to the realisation that you and your circle have improved as people.

I’m proud to present the “Book Donation drive for Tribal Child-2023” as a part of “Books and Beyond Library” to empower education in rural areas of our country. Clean off your bookshelf or clean off your wallets and help provide books for children. We collect any book suitable for children. The right book will expand your knowledge and will boost your mood. Drop a mail to sukeshrpillai@yahoo.com or drop your used or new books to Books N Beyond Library.
