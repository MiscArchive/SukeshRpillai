About Me

I’m Sukesh Ramakrishna Pillai, an author. The founder of Love All Sports Academy and BNB Library, Trivandrum. I’m an electronics and communications engineer with a master's degree in business administration and eighteen years of telecom industry experience. I have worked with prominent international conglomerates such as Reliance, Motorola, Ericsson, Wipro, and Globacom both in and out of India. As a sports fanatic, establishing a Center of Sports Excellence for popular games in Trivandrum was always my dream, and it was successfully launched on October 20, 2016. In 2022, I set up a free, open library in Trivandrum where anyone could visit and read without having to pay a membership fee, and so "BNB Library" (https://www.bnblibrary.in/) was born. The library is packed with a wide diversity of books on various topics and subjects. The objective of the library is to inculcate reading habits in all members of the public who have lost touch with reading due to the emergence of social media.

Books N Beyond

“Books N Beyond” library is the place you can walk straight to without the effort of maintaining a membership. Reading a book is completely free here. You can just walk in, read the book and give it back when you leave. If you wish to take the book home, you need to furnish a security amount. But that will duly be returned when the book is given back. “Books N Beyond” is here for those who love reading books- the right place for book lovers.
